# PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD): SOVEREIGN ECOSYSTEM

## 1. VISION & OVERVIEW
The **Sovereign Ecosystem** expands the GANI HYPHA autonomous economy into the physical and personal realms. It bridges the gap between digital Web3/Web5 sovereignty and real-world daily life through two primary pillars:
1.  **Sovereign Barber (SB):** A tech-forward, AI-integrated grooming experience that treats the barber shop as a community node and a data-driven business.
2.  **Sovereign Legacy (SL):** A digital and physical sanctuary for the family, automating home management and securing the family's "legacy" (data, assets, and history) through self-sovereign technology.

---

## 2. SOVEREIGN BARBER (SB) — "The Community Node"

### 2.1 Problem Statement
Traditional barber shops suffer from inefficient booking, lack of client data retention (styles, preferences), and manual financial tracking. They are often disconnected from the broader digital economy.

### 2.2 Solution
An AI-first barber management system integrated with the GANI HYPHA marketplace, offering:
- **AI Visual Consultation:** Analyzes face shape and hair type to suggest styles.
- **Dynamic Booking:** Web3/Web5-enabled scheduling that optimizes for the barber's peak hours and the client's "Sovereign" status.
- **Client Style Vault:** Stores historical cuts (photos and notes) as private, user-owned data (DWN).

### 2.3 Key Features
| Feature | Description | Tech Integration |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **AI Stylist Agent** | WhatsApp-based agent that handles bookings and style advice. | Groq (Llama 3.3), n8n |
| **Style NFT/Badge** | Clients earn "Proof of Grooming" badges for loyalty and status. | Alchemy, Supabase |
| **Inventory Automation** | AI predicts when products (pomade, blades) run low and auto-orders. | GANI Store API |
| **Sovereign Payment** | Accepts $HYPHA, stablecoins, and fiat with instant settlement. | Midtrans, Web3 Bridge |

## 3. SOVEREIGN LEGACY (SL) — "The Family Sanctuary"

### 3.1 Problem Statement
Family data (wills, photos, financial records) is scattered across centralized platforms (Google, iCloud) with no clear succession plan or privacy-first management. Home automation is often intrusive and data-leaky.

### 3.2 Solution
A "Digital Vault" and "Home OS" that centralizes family sovereignty, ensuring data longevity and automated household operations.

### 3.3 Key Features
| Feature | Description | Tech Integration |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Legacy Vault** | Encrypted storage for essential documents (Wills, Deeds, IDs). | Pinata (IPFS), Web5 DWN |
| **Family Treasury** | Shared dashboard for family finances, savings, and $HYPHA staking. | Supabase, Web3 Wallet |
| **Sovereign Home IoT** | Privacy-first home automation (security, energy, maintenance). | Local-first IoT, Web5 |
| **Succession Protocol** | Automated "Dead Man's Switch" or multi-sig for legacy transfer. | Smart Contracts (Hypha) |

## 5. INFINITY LOOP & CIRCULATING ECONOMY INTEGRATION

### 5.1 The "Infinity Loop" Principle
Both Sovereign Barber and Sovereign Legacy will operate on an "Infinity Loop" model, inspired by subathon mechanics. This means the ecosystem is designed to be self-sustaining, where user engagement and transactions (the "DNA") directly fuel its continuous operation and evolution.

-   **Sovereign Barber:** Every booking, service, or product purchase contributes to the barber shop's operational "timer." High engagement can unlock new features, discounts, or community events.
-   **Sovereign Legacy:** Active management of family assets, regular data backups, and participation in the GANI HYPHA ecosystem extends the "lifespan" and security features of the Legacy Vault and Home OS.

### 5.2 Circulating Economy (The Engine)
Value generated within one part of the Sovereign Ecosystem will circulate to support other parts, primarily driven by the $HYPHA token.

-   **Barber Shop Revenue:** A portion of profits from Sovereign Barber services will be automatically directed to the Family Treasury within Sovereign Legacy, strengthening family finances.
-   **GANI Store Integration:** Purchases of supplies for both the barber shop and the smart home through the GANI Store will utilize $HYPHA, creating a continuous demand and reward cycle.
-   **Staking & Rewards:** Users staking $HYPHA will receive benefits across both platforms, further incentivizing participation and locking value within the ecosystem.
